Ta'ameya (Egyptian Falafel)

Description

Ta'ameya, or Egyptian Falafel is an all time favorite street food, best served with pita bread, tomato, onions, and tahini sauce.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups dried split fava beans
  • 1 red onion, quartered
  • 1/2 cup fresh parsley
  • 1/2 cup fresh cilantro
  • 1/2 cup fresh dill
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons ground coriander
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 cup sesame seeds (Optional)
  • vegetable oil for frying

Steps

  1. Soak fava beans in a large bowl of water for minimum 8hrs
  2. Combine soaked fava beans, red onion, parsley, cilantro, dill, garlic coriander, salt, and cumin in a food processor, and grind the mixture to a dough-like consistency
  3. Heat a skillet over medium heat. Add sesame seeds, cook, stirring occasionally, until toasted, about 5mins. Transfer to a large plate
  4. Shape fava bean mixture into balls. Roll in sesame seeds to coat
  5. Heat 1/4 full saucepan over medium heat. Fry fava bean balls in batches until golden brown (3-5mins). Drain on paper towels.
