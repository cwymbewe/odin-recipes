Pandan Chiffon Cake

Description

Pandan cake is a fluffy, gorgeous green-coloured chiffon cake made with aromatic South East Asian pandan paste

Ingredients

  • 6 eggs, separated
  • 1/4 teaspoon cream of tartar
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 8 tablespoons water
  • 1/4 teaspoon pandan paste
  • 5 tablespoons corn oil
  • 1 cup self-rising flour, sifted

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 165 Deg C
  2. Combine egg whites and cream of tartar in the bowl of a stand mixer fitted with the whisk attachment; beat egg whites until stiff peaks form
  3. Beat egg yolks and sugar in a separate bowl until sugar has dissolved
  4. Whisk water and pandan paste until smooth; mix thoroughly into egg yolk mixture. Stir in corn, then fold in sifted flour
  5. Fold egg yolk mixture gently into beaten egg whites using a spatula. Pour into a 9-inch chiffon cake mold
  6. Bake in th preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the centre comes out clean, 45-50 minutes. Invert cake mold onto a cooling rack immediately after removing it from the oven. Let cake cool upside down in the mold. Loosen cooled cake from the mold using a sharp knife
